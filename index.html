What is Support Your Local Salon?

As a supporter of the Salon & Spa Industry in Ireland, Digital Salon have launched an initiative to encourage clients to support their local salon. 'SupportYourLocalSalon.ie' is a website setup to help drive new customers to salons who are selling products online. The website is non-profit making and is free of charge for Salons who want to list their online Shop. Visitors to the site will be able to search for the nearest Salon selling products online and click into their online shop. The Website is being promoted across Google and Social media, all of which is paid for by our very generous sponsors. Digital Salon are the only company in the country specialising in helping Salons and Spas with their online presence. It’s hoped that by launching and promoting this new website, Digital Salon can give something back to the industry by encouraging people all over the country to ‘Support their local Salon’.
